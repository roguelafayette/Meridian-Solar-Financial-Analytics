
# Meridian Solar Financial Performance Analysis

Multi-project margin and cost analysis for staffing industry workforce 
financial management. Analyzes 1.08M labor hours across 5 active 
projects over a 30-month reporting window, surfacing margin dynamics, 
cost concentration, and craft-level performance.

## 📊 Project Overview

This dashboard addresses how to surface margin performance across a 
multi-project portfolio when strategic pricing decisions create variance 
from industry benchmarks. It enables executives to distinguish between 
operational underperformance and deliberate business trade-offs — a 
distinction generic industry benchmarks would obscure.

### Analytical Approach

- **Dual scoping methodology** — Financial metrics tie to P&L totals; 
  operational metrics isolate true labor activity
- **Portfolio-calibrated benchmarks** — Health-indicator thresholds 
  set against the actual 1.43x Regular / 1.41x OT markup structure 
  rather than generic industry averages
- **OT cost decomposition** — Isolates premium cost, markup sacrifice, 
  and cost concentration to enable granular pricing analysis
- **Anonymization framework** — Preserves analytical relationships 
  while removing all identifying information

## 🎯 Key Findings

- **OT concentration at 15%** — Above the 10% industry benchmark; 
  driven by California overtime laws on Coyote Flats and Sage Valley's 
  aggressive ramp phase

- **Portfolio margin at 22.24%** — Reflects strategic pricing on the 
  Meridian Solar flagship relationship, the portfolio's highest 
  revenue generator

- **Crew Lead margin at 29%** — Outperforms portfolio average by 7 
  points despite limited per-site positions; opportunity to 
  specialize staffing across other projects to maximize margin capture

- **Travel OT impact minimal** — $4,868 (0.04% of portfolio margin) 
  at current volumes; instrumented for scaling visibility

- **OT markup sacrifice quantified** — Approximately $151K annually 
  from reduced 1.41x OT markup vs. 1.43x Regular structure, 
  concentrated primarily on Pine Star ($82K)

## ⚖️ Note on Data

All data, entities, project names, and identifiers are illustrative 
and representative of industry patterns. Employee names, financial 
values, and organizational references have been transformed to remove 
identifying information while preserving analytical relationships. 
Aggregate hours are preserved as-recorded; financial values are 
scaled by a consistent multiplier.
